Computers are capable of many things. They are already used in many areas of human activity. For example medicine. Thanks to computers, people with disabilities can control various devices with the help of one organ such as the eye. They help other people to replace some senses with others. For example, replace the image with sound. Computers are also used in the police, police terminals have many useful functions: a database, fingerprints, a camera, a microphone and much more.
